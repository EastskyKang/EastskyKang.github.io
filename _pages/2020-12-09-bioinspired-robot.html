We (the Computational Robotics Lab) build bio-inspired quadrupedal robots that resemble beloved animals. Our research projects explore the challenge of designing mechanisms and control strategies that can generate more animal-like walking motion for legged robots.

Our research topics are broad-ranging from control and planning of legged robots to design optimization, digital fabrication and finally human-robot interaction.

We are looking for talented students who are passionate to join our journey to build a bio-inspired legged robot. Please contact us if you are intested in participating in one of our 3-month (14-weeks, part-time) or 6-month (full-time) research projects at Computational Robotics Lab.

For students from research or educational institutes other than ETH

The Master's semester project and thesis project are part of the Master's degree program at ETH Zurich and we cannot provide any financial support for cost of living and neither legal support for your visa process. However, there are plenty of opportunities to visit ETH for research exchanging purposes. So please make sure you can visit ETH and Switzerland with legitimated permission supported by one of these programmes before you contact us.

  • Exchange and visiting studies
  • ETH Student Summer Research Fellowship (Department of Computer Science)
  • ETH Robotics Student Fellowship
